Privacy Policy

Push Your Reps - Fitness
Last updated: February 22, 2026

BossInfotech ("we", "our", or "us") built the Push Your Reps - Fitness app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

When you use Push Your Reps, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Google account name, email address, and profile picture when you sign in with Google.
  • Fitness Data: Workout logs, exercise data, nutrition entries, water intake, body measurements, and menstrual cycle data that you voluntarily enter.
  • Profile Information: Name, age, gender, height, weight, and fitness goals that you provide.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's fitness tracking features
  • Sync your data across devices using your Google account
  • Calculate BMI, calorie targets, and workout suggestions
  • Store your data securely in Firebase Cloud Firestore

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use Firebase Authentication for secure sign-in and Firebase Firestore for cloud data storage.

Your fitness data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and synced to Firebase cloud storage linked to your Google account.

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your health and fitness data remains private and is only accessible by you through your Google account.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app (Settings > Delete Account).

Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account and all data from Settings > Delete Account within the app. This action is irreversible and will remove all your workout history, nutrition logs, measurements, and profile information from both local storage and cloud servers.

Children's Privacy

Push Your Reps is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, cloud data storage (Firebase Privacy Policy)
  • Google Sign-In: Account authentication (Google Privacy Policy)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
